Team Ventures

The Team Ventures of GECCS is passionately committed to advancing electric mobility and delving into the forefront of electric vehicle (EV) technology. Recent years have seen the team achieve significant milestones, earning acclaim and accolades across diverse competitions and events. Engaging in practical projects, workshops, and seminars, the team consistently drives forward with their research and initiatives. Their unwavering dedication is geared towards fostering societal progress and molding a brighter tomorrow.In the year 2023-24 alone, Team Ventures achieved remarkable success across various platforms. They emerged as the Winner of the District level competition Maharashtra State Innovation Challenges 2024, organized by MSI, showcasing their prowess in innovative solutions within the state. Their triumph continued with a First Prize win at the CMIA 2024 Paper Presentation Competition, underscoring their expertise in presenting cutting-edge research and ideas.

Further solidifying their reputation, Team Ventures secured the First Prize in the State Level business idea presentation competition 2024 held at Guru Govind Singh College, Nashik. This accomplishment highlighted their ability to not only conceive innovative business concepts but also effectively communicate them to a discerning audience.

The team's entrepreneurial spirit shone through as they clinched the Second Prize in the Start-up Competition 2024 at KBT College, Nashik, showcasing their capacity to translate ideas into viable startup ventures. Additionally, they claimed the prestigious First Prize in the India Innovates Competition Wings 24, a testament to their creative approach and problem-solving acumen in the realm of technological innovation.

Beyond competitions, Team Ventures also dedicated their time to inspiring future generations by visiting Sharda Vidya Mandir School to promote enthusiasm for engineering among school students. This initiative exemplifies their commitment to not only achieving academic and entrepreneurial success but also nurturing a passion for innovation in the wider community.

As Team Ventures of GECCS continues to push the boundaries of electric mobility and EV technology through hands-on projects, workshops, and seminars, their impact on society remains profound. Their collective efforts are aimed at shaping a better future, where technological innovation plays a pivotal role in driving positive change.
