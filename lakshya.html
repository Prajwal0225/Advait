Lakshya: A Sporting Saga




The Lakshya Sports Week at Government College of Engineering, Aurangabad, was more than just a competition; it was a vibrant celebration of youth, unity, and athletic prowess. From the moment the inaugural carrom shot was played by our esteemed Principal, the stage was set for a week-long extravaganza that would leave an enduring mark on the college's annals.

Under the expert stewardship of Dr. S. D. Ambekar, Prof. S. P. Atipamulu, Dr. Shyam Sonawane, Dr. Vipul Pawar, and Vishant Charak, the event was meticulously orchestrated, ensuring a seamless experience for all participants. The college grounds transformed into a battleground of sorts, yet one where the spirit of camaraderie reigned supreme.

Students from various departments, including Civil, Mechanical, Electrical, Electronics & Telecommunication, Information Technology, Computer Science Engineering, MCA, and M.Tech, showcased their athletic finesse across a diverse spectrum of sports. The thrill of victory and the agony of defeat were intertwined, forging bonds of friendship and respect among the participants.

The indoor games of carrom, badminton, table tennis, and chess tested the players' precision and mental acuity, while the outdoor sports of cricket, kabaddi, kho-kho, volleyball, basketball, and football demanded physical prowess, teamwork, and strategic acumen. The tug-of-war and athletics events were a spectacle of strength, speed, and endurance, leaving the audience spellbound.

Beyond the competitive spirit, Lakshya served as a platform for personal growth and character development. Students learned the importance of discipline, perseverance, and teamwork, qualities that are invaluable in both academic and professional pursuits. The event fostered a sense of belonging and camaraderie, creating lasting memories that will be cherished for years to come.

The Lakshya Sports Week culminated in a grand prize distribution ceremony on March 15th, presided over by the esteemed Chief Guest, Shri Vikram Sali. The ceremony was a fitting finale, honoring the exceptional achievements of the winning teams and individuals.

As the curtains fell on this remarkable event, Lakshya left an indelible imprint on the hearts and minds of all involved. It was a testament to the indomitable spirit of the GECA community and a celebration of the power of sports to unite people. The success of Lakshya 2024 is a testament to the hard work and dedication of the organizing committee, the enthusiastic participation of the students, and the unwavering support of the college administration.

Interview Bhalchandra Nemade H.C. Verma Dr. Vijay Bhatkar
