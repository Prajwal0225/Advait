The Annual Cultural Fest!

Wings Antarang

Antarang




The Government College of Engineering, Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar ,Aurangabad (GECA) is not just a hub of academic excellence but also a vibrant center of cultural activities. Every year, the campus buzzes with excitement as it gears up for "Antarang," the college's annual cultural festival. This vibrant celebration brings together students, faculty, and alumni to showcase talent, creativity, and a spirit of unity. The fest is not only a platform for artistic expression but also a testament to the rich cultural diversity and enthusiasm of the college community.

Day 1 sought the presentation of “Shobhayatra”, This unique event celebrated the diversity of cultures across the globe by representing multiple countries of the G-20 summit. The inauguration program of Antarang 2024, the annual cultural event of Government College of Engineering, themed "Vasudhaiva Kutumbakam," commenced on February 29, 2024, at the Basketball Court. The event was marked by the esteemed presence of notable dignitaries, including Shri Hrushikesh Ranade ( renowned singer and musician) as the chief guest, along with the college principal Dr. S. S. Dambhare sir, faculty members, and members of the Student Council.Then “Mr. & Mrs. GECA” competition was done .This competition highlights the importance of well-rounded development. The crowning of winners happened.The singing competition opened with a melodious performance from the chief guest, Hrishikesh Ranade, setting the tone for an evening of musical brilliance. Day 1 was complete by the Kalarpan concert, held as a vibrant and lively event, showcased several popular Bollywood songs, captivating the audience with the talents of the performers

Day 2 of the annual cultural fest, themed Bollywood Day, featured a variety of performances including Monoact, Stand-up Comedy, Poetry, Open Mic, Mimicry, Antakshari,Dance competitions and finally ended with Classical Evening-“Saptrang”.These events served as a platform to celebrate different art forms, uniting students,faculty, and artists in a shared celebration of talent and creativity.The "AajaNachle" dance competition, held as part of Antarang 2024 was a resounding success. It attracted a diverse range of talented participants from various departments of the college, providing a platform for dancers to showcase their skills, creativity, and passion for dance. The classical evening witnessed enthusiastic participation from every department. The participants showcased a diverse array of performances, including Kathak, Bharatnatyam, classical vocal solos, and instrumental fusion, highlighting the rich cultural tapestry and artistic talents prevalent among college students.

Day 3 was the last day of Antarang 2024.The triumph of the "Musical Skit" competition was evident in its ability to attract a varied pool of talented participants from diverse college departments. Acting as a dynamic stage, the event allowed participants to unveil their prowess, explore inventive expressions, and display an unbridled devotion to the art of acting. lunch was organized for all the students, and everyone enjoyed it immensely.In “Qawwali” competition the participants showcased a diverse array of qawwali performances, including classical and Sufi singing, highlighting the rich cultural talent prevalent among the college students.Antarang 2024 got to its end with a prize distribution ceremony where prizes for all the competitions held during the three-day cultural fest were distributed. The event also featured the prize distribution among students who participated and won at ICT 2023-2024. All winners and runners-up were felicitated by the dignitaries. At the end of this, the Computer Science Engineering Department, which won the most prizes, was declared the best department and awarded the shield. Additionally, the best outgoing student was declared and felicitated.

College authorities and all the students lead the program in such a disciplinary manner that it stood as an example for best cooperation and maximum participation.
