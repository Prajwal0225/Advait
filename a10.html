You are not alone in crowd……. you are the Special one.

In this huge universe we live in there are plenty of things that make us sometimes happy and sometimes sad. All of us are in different phases of our life with each phase filled with different sufferings and happy moments. This all always trying to teach us that every day is not the same for anybody. Everyone is in their race and trying to win it. But the harsh truth is only 1% will going to be winner. But what about the rest 99%. If You unfortunately failed then the question will always will be there “Oh God, why always me? I gave my best.” If God could answer then he also would have said that “It is not the time yet, don’t you lose hope and try harder next time.” But after trying many times again and again then also you failed then the answer you got previously get vanishes because you have lost all the hopes.

When you lose then you get to feel that I am the worst one, I cannot do anything good in my life. But sometimes the perspective about these things is also important. If you will try to break concrete wall with your hand then it is obvious that you will always fail. So, you always need to understand firstly for what exactly you are made for. Materialistic thing or a high-status post in any organisation or company should not be your goal. The final motive should be being happy in it what you do. Not every passion needs to be get monetized. Somethings you should do the things for happiness of your soul.

“If you judge a fish by aim to climb a mountain then that Fish will spend whole life has a loser only”
– Albert Einstein

Persons with the same dream cannot have same life. Each person varies with other person in multiple aspect because everyone has lived their life in a different way. Every one of us are unique and special one in different ways. So, you do not need compare yourself with other on basis of a single competition or race. If you cannot do one thing after multiple attempts then leave it because you can do other 3 things better than that one. So, whenever you feel that I am alone and stuck in illusive know where then remind every time to yourself that you are the special one that why you are alone.

-Pranay Ingle
