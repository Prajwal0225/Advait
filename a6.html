Sentinelese ; Genuinely Isolated Yet United !

In the heart of Bay of Bengal the island that remained dwelling in its own way, away from the clutches of modernization , exist world’s most isolated island – A coral-fringed Island known as North sentinel is one of the Andaman’s Island.

No people on earth remain more genuinely isolated than Sentinelese ! Sentinelese is the uncontacted tribe that lives on North sentinel Island. A tribe that remained unchanged for ten thousands of years . Any attempts made to contact them have been met with showers of spears and arrows as the inhabitants have made it aptly clear that they want to be left alone. Thus it is believed that the Sentinelese is the most dangerous , isolated and hostile tribe.

Many attempt were made before to contact Sentinelese but they were welcomed by spheres and arrows , but on 4th January 1991 finally the day arose when the ice of hostility and the isolation was broken by the oneness or the unity of humanity , love and affection by the young woman anthropologist named Madhumala Chattopadhyay -the woman who built first bridge to the unknown world . This was the first ever friendly contact made with this hostile but humanized tribe.

It was the expedition carried out by the Anthropological survey of India to make friendly contact with the hostile tribes of Andaman. The crew of 13 people were sent to the Sentinel with the single woman member , their ship anchored off on the southwestern coast of North sentinel where they saw three huts but the coast was deserted. They saw smoke was coming out from the another part of Island and they turned the way of their boat towards that direction , suddenly the Sentinelese men with very few women came out behind the trees with arrows and spheres in their hand but this time they did not directly attacked as they saw woman on the boat which indicated to Sentinelese that the boat people mean no harm , as the over protective towards their women and children which reflected the oneness of care and love towards our beloved ones among this most primitive and so called modern people of today’s world .Now it was the contact team of Madhumala to take initiative. They had carried coconuts with them in their boat. They started dropping coconuts in the water one by one.

Then something that had never been seen before happened , some Sentinelese men came forward and started collecting coconuts .After few hours of this event Madhumala with brave heart decided to jump into the water and she went towards the shore and the coconuts were given hand to hand , the communication without words and sentences but with gestures and love and affection begin between the Sentinelese and Madhumala . At this moment it reflected the humanity of this most secluded and hostile tribe of the world who did not harm this brave lady. And the crew returned back safely.

Next time when Madhumala went Sentinel in February the Sentinelese welcomed her warm heartedly and enthusiastically. It showed the oneness among the humans , through the love and care . It reflected however the Sentinelese are isolated , primitive and hostile towards outsiders they are united by humanity.

-Ketaki Randive
