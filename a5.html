The engineer

Being an engineer is one of the most unparalleled professions to ever exist!! No matter which core and branch , an engineer is Jack of all trades. Engineers are the invisible bridges of human society, they innovate new ideas and bring out technology to enhance lives of people.

An engineers mind is expected to be extremely creative, having a whole set of qualities like problem solving, knowing STEM subject applications ,being practical and resourceful, and the never ending list of skills mentioned in a job description.

An engineer is a person who will be a hardworking ant and a lazy grasshopper both in same body . They will procrastinate until the end of deadline and still somehow get their jobs done ! Engineering degrees were definately invented to form a strong man force of educated class of people that could solve modern world problem and come up with the best solution, rather than just mock and harass every other major.

Every engineering grad has tasted bitter potion made out of struggle and hard work, no matter where he might stand in his future .

An engineer might spend days and hours thinking about a problem, getting a reliable solution just for it to be ''why didn't i think about this earlier'' and then getting as excited as Archimedes, this is probably little too much but engineers know how to build a roof of happiness from a fragile pillar of joy. Engineering includes designing a pen that works without friction in space and engineering also includes using a wooden graphite pencil to write on paper in space . It also includes writing a plus C after solving every intergral when they don't know answers, hoping to get the partial marking they lose otherwise. A normal person might know English and Hindi alphabets, but an engineer has already learnt greek alphabets, an unappreciated skill by many .

Talent could easily be founded among engineers, just a little bit of Kickstart and an engineer will amaze you like nothing else. An engineer will always stand out from the croud, unless entire croud consists of engineers!

Over the years of experience engineers gain throughout their lifespan, they are always learning and evolving, which makes them unique and exceptional in their own significant way.


- Isha Sangnale
