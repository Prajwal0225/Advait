2) Maharashtra Student Innovation Challenge (MSIC)

Maharashtra State Innovation Society (MSINS) is a nodal government agency to boost innovation-driven entrepreneurial ecosystem in the state of Maharashtra. It is established under Skills, Employment, Entrepreneurship and Innovation Department, Government of Maharashtra, the society aims to foster innovative approaches and create conducive environment for innovative businesses to operate in Maharashtra.



A   Round 1 -Maharashtra State Innovation Challenge by EDC at Institute Level


Maharashtra Student Innovation Challenge is an initiative to hunt young entrepreneurs and support them with possible support to achieve their startup dreams. Students from the institute were asked to submit their ideas through MSINS portal. Seven teams participated from the institute. Two teams were selected for final round. Among the innovative ideas put forth by the students, there were concepts ranging from sustainable technology solutions to social entrepreneurship initiatives. One team proposed a smart helmet for riders that aimed to optimize safety in riding processes and reduce accidental impact, while another presented a student learning platform for connecting local industries with a skilled student, digital learning in students.

B) MSIC Finals at CSMSS College of Engineering Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar‍

The final round of Maharashtra State Innovation Challenge of the district was conducted at CSMSS dated 15/12/2023.  The event, organized at the state level, served as a platform for students to demonstrate their creative and problem-solving skills. Two teams of students from GECA-CSN participated in the finals.                              

B) MSIC Winners get 1 lakh seed funding:‍


Two teams participated in the district-level competition, showcasing their innovative ideas and solutions to address various challenges faced by society. As a reward for their efforts, each team was granted Rs 1 lakh in seed funding to further develop and implement their innovative ideas.

Winning Teams

Team 1: Team Master Innovators (Learnify – Student Learning Platform) – Rushikesh Chabukswar and team members
Team 2: Team Ventures (Smart Helmet) – Harish Chaudhari and team membersBoth teams emerged victorious in the competition, demonstrating exceptional creativity and innovation in their projects. As a reward for their efforts, each team was granted Rs 1 lakh in seed funding to further develop and implement their innovative ideas.   

On Republic Day, 26th January 2024, winning teams were felicitated at the Police Commissioner Office in Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar. The ceremony was attended by Police Commissioner, Guardian Minster of Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar, who commended the teams for their remarkable achievements and contributions to innovation.

3. My Story: Motivational session By Successful Entrepreneur

On January 16, 2024, the Government College of Engineering, Aurangabad, Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar, welcomed back one of its esteemed alumni, Mr. Shivprasad Jaju, CEO of Pawan Groups, for an interactive talk filled with personal anecdotes, insights into entrepreneurship, and reflections on his college and mechanical engineering journey.Mr. Jaju shared his own experiences and challenges faced during his college and professional journey, offering the realities of entrepreneurship. His self-stories resonated with the audience, inspiring them to embrace challenges and pursue their goals with passion and determination. His journey from student to successful entrepreneur served as a testament to the transformative power of education, perseverance, and passion.

4.  National Entrepreneurship Challenge (NEC) IITB

The National Entrepreneurship Challenge (NEC), organized by E-cell IIT Bombay at the national level, has proven to be a transformative experience for our students. This six-month-long competition is a competitive, comprehensive development platform.Students actively participated in the NEC, representing our institution with enthusiasm and dedication. The competition provided an excellent opportunity to showcase its organizational skills, entrepreneurial spirit, and commitment to fostering innovation and leadership among students. The competition not only hones strategic thinking but also enhances the ability to navigate the dynamic landscape of entrepreneurship.
