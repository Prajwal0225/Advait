Team Cybrotics

Team Cybrotics, based at the Government College of Engineering Aurangabad, Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar, has had an impressive year in 2024. This group of robotics enthusiasts, known for their expertise and innovation, has been fabricating robots since 2010. Their commitment to excellence is evident through their continued participation in the ABU-ROBOCON, an esteemed international robotic contest. The national level of this competition, organized by Doordarshan, offers the winners a chance to represent India on the international stage. Beyond competitions, Team Cybrotics actively contributes to the academic community through the RAISE Society (Robotics Association and Innovation Society), where they host workshops on robotics and automation to foster young talent from various academic disciplines.

In 2024, the theme for DD-ROBOCON was "Harvest Day," focusing on the integration of robotic technologies in agricultural processes such as planting, cultivating, and harvesting crops to enhance efficiency and productivity. Team Cybrotics performed exceptionally well in this competition. In January 2024, during Stage 1, they achieved an All-India Rank (AIR) of 5 with an impressive score of 96/100. Their success continued into Stage 2, where they secured an AIR of 4 with a score of 94/100. Currently, the team is diligently working on Stage 3 of the competition, demonstrating their relentless pursuit of excellence.

In addition to their achievements in DD-ROBOCON, Team Cybrotics has excelled in various Robo Race competitions throughout the year. At Innovo’24, held at Shri Sant Gajanan Maharaj College of Engineering in Shegaon, the team secured the 1st rank in the RC Car Racing category. They replicated this success at Takshntra, hosted by Shreeyash College of Engineering & Technology in Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar, where they also achieved the 1st rank in RC Car Racing. Furthermore, at WINGS 2k24, Team Cybrotics distinguished themselves by securing both the 1st and 2nd runner-up positions in the Robo-Race category, showcasing their depth of talent and competitive spirit.

Team Cybrotics also made a notable impact at the India Innovates competition, where they demonstrated their innovative prowess by attaining the 1st runner-up position. This achievement highlights their capacity for creativity and problem-solving in the rapidly evolving field of robotics.

Throughout 2024, Team Cybrotics has upheld their tradition of excellence in robotics. Their performance in various high-profile competitions underscores their technical acumen, innovation, and competitive spirit. As they continue to advance and share their knowledge, they remain a formidable presence in the robotics field, inspiring future generations of roboticists and pushing the boundaries of technological possibility.
