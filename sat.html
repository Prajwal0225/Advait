SAT Club

The Student Alumni Team (SAT) at Government Engineering College Aurangabad,  Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar, serves as a crucial platform for connecting current students with  accomplished alumni. Under the leadership of President AAGECA, Shri Shrikant Umarikar,  and faculty advisor Prof. Dhote, SAT is dedicated to empowering students both academically  and personally. Its mission is focused on providing essential resources and support to deserving  individuals, ensuring they have the tools necessary to excel in their academic and professional  journeys. Vishant Charak is the head of SAT, driving initiatives to connect students and alumni  effectively.

SAT has launched a variety of impactful initiatives aimed at fostering connections between  students and alumni. These initiatives include a scholarship program, the Annual Alumni Meet,  and the Rare Share Program, among others. The team also plays a vital role in assisting various  student clubs in securing budgets and sponsorships from alumni, while recognizing outstanding  achievements within the student body.

One of the most anticipated events organized by SAT is the Annual Alumni Meet, which took  place on March 10, 2024. This gathering served not only as a reunion but also as an opportunity  to celebrate shared memories, professional exchanges, and remarkable achievements. The  event commenced with an air of anticipation, featuring esteemed guests such as Mr. N.D.  Deshmukh and Mr. Jayant Deo, alongside Mr. Shrikant Umarikar, the President of the Alumni  Association (AAGECA),the college's Principal, Dr. Sanjay Dambhare, the Vice President Dr.  Uttam Kalwane and the Secretary Dr.Sanjay Shinde.

The meet was meticulously crafted to facilitate knowledge exchange and professional  networking. Panel discussions featuring successful alumni from diverse engineering disciplines  were a focal point, bridging the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical realities.  Alumni shared their career journeys, challenges faced, and valuable lessons learned, offering  inspiration and guidance to the next generation of engineers.

The evening culminated in a prestigious felicitation ceremony, recognizing distinguished  graduates who have made significant impacts in their fields. Notably, Pritam Nikumbh was  honored as the Best Outgoing Student, and Mayuri Sangekar received accolades for her thirdyear performance. Teams like Aryans and Cyberotics were celebrated for their outstanding  performances in various competitions, reinforcing the college’s commitment to excellence. Team Kalarpan members were also lauded for their stellar performance in Firodia Karandak  and Purushottam Karandak. Mr. Bhushan Borde Patil and his team were then recognized for  their exceptional performance in the Taksha Antra competition, further amplifying the  evening's spirit of achievement. Finally, to ensure a complete recognition of all contributors,  the ceremony concluded with the felicitation of Mr. Vishant Charak, head of the student-alumni  team, for his exceptional contribution.

Additionally, the cultural program added a festive touch to the event, featuring captivating  performances by students and alumni. The enchanting Ganesh Vandana by Nandini Khandarey  set a tone of traditional elegance, while other performances showcased the rich talent within  the college community.

The inauguration ceremony for the SAT team in 2023 was held on October 23, 2023, at the IT  Seminar Hall of the college. Dr. A.S. Bhalachandra, the former Principal, served as the chief  guest, sharing invaluable insights with attendees. Alongside her were esteemed guests  including Mr. Shrikant Umarikar, President of AAGECA, Mr. Vyanktesh Dhote, Dr. Uttam  Kalwane, and Mr. S.M. Shinde, all of whom symbolized the enduring bond between the college  and its alumni. The event included a compelling presentation that outlined the aspirations and  plans for SAT in the coming year. Team members were honored for their dedication,  embodying the spirit of leadership and innovation.

Beyond formalities, the ceremony provided an opportunity for networking, fostering  camaraderie among attendees. This event marked a significant beginning for the new SAT  team, symbolizing the harmonious convergence of past and present and paving the way for  future collaborations.

On September 9, 2023, SAT organized "Umang," a vibrant event celebrating culture and  spirituality in conjunction with AAGECA, EVOLVE CLUB, and Kalarpan. This event  coincided with Gokulashtami and showcased a myriad of cultural expressions through  captivating dance and music performances. Dr. Rohinipriya Das delivered an inspiring speech  that set the tone for the evening, enhancing the program's depth and significance.

The interactions between students and alumni during "Umang" fostered a strong sense of  community, emphasizing the importance of continuity and collaboration within the college  environment. This event was a testament to the creativity and talent present within the college,  leaving a lasting impact on all participants.

The "Alumni Meet Pune Chapter," held on September 4, 2023, further strengthened the bonds  between alumni and the college. The event commenced with the warm welcome of esteemed  chief guests, Shri Jayant Dev (MCRC) and Shri B. M. Domkundwar, alongside the President,  Mr. Shrikant Umrikar, Secretary, Dr. Sanjay Shinde, and Mr. Suresh Tandale at 12:00 pm. Mr.  Shrikant Umrikar, President of AAGECA, underscored the significance of the event, followed  by the felicitation of the guests of honor. Vishant Charak, Head of SAT, highlighted alumni  contributions to the team's management, reinforcing the collaborative spirit that defines the  college community.

Dr. Sanjay Shinde, Secretary of AAGECA, shed light on the profound impact GECA has had  on its students over the years, attributing much of this success to the generous contributions of  the alumni. The guests of honor, Shri Jayant Dev and Shri B. M. Domkundwar, imparted words  of wisdom for the alumni, emphasizing the importance of carrying forward the values and  traditions instilled during their time at the institution. Mr. Shrikant Umrikar shared his personal  experiences and extended gratitude for the continuous support of the alumni.

Through its diverse initiatives and events, the Student Alumni Team at Government  Engineering College Aurangabad is more than just a bridge between current students and  alumni; it embodies the collaborative spirit essential for academic and personal growth. With  dedicated leadership and active participation from both students and alumni, SAT continues to  foster a thriving community, ensuring that the legacy of excellence at the college endures. The  impact of these efforts resonates far beyond the college walls, shaping the futures of countless  students and strengthening the bonds of the alumni network.
