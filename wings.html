The Annual Technical Fest!

Wings Antarang

Wings




Date: 19/01/2024 to 21/01/2024

Session Details

Every year the Government College of Engineering, Chh. Sambhajinagar holds a national level technical event for the students of all over India to showcase their knowledge and skills. This year, the grand event Wings was arranged as a three day long grand event from 19th to 21st of January, 2024. During this event many seminars, competitions and guest lectures were arranged for the benefits of the students. Given below is the detailed report of Wings 2024.

Day 1:

Inauguration Ceremony:
The first day of Wings 2024 started with the Inauguration Ceremony which was held at 11 am tov12 am. Around 200 students were present for the ceremony. The ceremony was attended by the Chief Guest G. Shrikanth, Guest of honor Sonalika Akhouri (regional officer, SBI, Chh.Sambhajinagar, Ravindra regionalShikhar Goel,  U. T. Nagdive, Prasad Kokil, Suraj Dumne, Dr. S. S. Dambhare, Dr. A. S. Bhalachandra, Dr. R. K. Shrivastav, Dr. S. B. Chikalthankar, Dr. S. B. Nemade, Prof. V. P. Gaikwad, Prof. S. P. Atipamulu. After the Inauguration, all the honorable guests were felicitated. Dr. A. S. Bhalachandra gave a summary of the upcoming three day event of Wings. The principal, Dr. Dambhare, inspired the students to participate in such events and shared his views on wanting to make Wings an international event. The chief guest, Mr. G. Shrikanth, shared some of his life experiences and motivated thr students to make breakthroughs in the field of engineering. Sonalika Akhouri ma’am also shared her inspiring life experiences with the students. Then Suraj Dumne, member of the Board of Management, shared how the board is immensely helpful to all the students. Nagdive sir also shared his valuable thoughts and experiences with the students. The vote of thanks was conducted by S. B. Nemade madam and the ceremony ended with the National anthem.




Keynote Session:
A keynote session of Shikhar Goel, Chief Technology Officer, geeksdforgeeks, was arranged for the sudents at 12 pm. Mr. Goel is a very talented and experienced person working in the firld of technology for the last 8 years. He has a very good command on his programming as well as management skills. He shared his journey from tier three to CTO with the students. The students were touched by hearing his story and the hardships he had to go through. He encouraged the students to keep experimenting, learning and experiencing new things as well as create new technology for the future generations.




Dronix:
Dronix is a national level drone competition designed to test the skills and drones of the participants. The first round was held on 2 pm to 5 pm. Total eight teams participated from all over Maharashtra.  Five out of the eight participants were shortlisted for the second round.




Ode to Code:
Ode to Code is a competition that tests the coding skills and capabilities of the participants. It was conducted at 2 pm to 5 pm at IT and CSE Departments. Total 150 students participated in the competition. Out of them, the top three winners were felicitated and rewarded.




Music Concert
An enthralling and energizing music concert was arranged for the students on the evening of 19th January, from 6:30 pm onwards. The Soor Nava Dhyaas Nava finalist, Munawar Ali and his band of 7 members were invited for this music concert. All the students as well as the faculty were present for the show.





Day 2:


Hackathon
The competition of Hackathon is hosted for students to showcase their hacking skills and capabilities. It was inaugurated by the principal Dr. Dambhare along with the guests Gaurav Admane and Gautam Choudhary at 8 pm. A total of 12 teams participated in Round 1 out of which 7 teams were selected for Round 2. The winners of the event were felicitated and rewarded. The event ended at 8pm on the same day.




Dronix Round II
The 2nd day of Wings started with the second round of Dronix which was held from 10:30 am tp 12:30 pm. The finalists from the first round competed in Round II and out of them, the top three winners were felicitated and rewarded.




Bike and Car Expo:
The bike and car expo was inaugurated by Dr. A. S. Bhalachandra on 20th January, 2024 at 1 pm. This expo exhibited various types and brands of luxury bikes and cars. A small procession of all the bikes and cars was carried out through the campus for the students to see.





India Innovates
India innovates is a competition cum exhibition of various projects made by students. It was held at 1:30 pm till 4:30 pm at Electronics Seminar Hall. Various teams from different colleges participated in the competition to showcase their innovative projects. A total of 18 teams participated in the competition where one team had four to six students each. The winners of the event were.




Technical Treasure Hunt:
The Technical Treasure Hunt was organized at Civil Department at 3:30 to 5:30 and was inaugurated by Dr. A S. Bhalachandra. A total of 50 teams participated in the competition for the first round out of which 12 teams went ahead into Round 2. For the third and final round, top six teams were shortlisted.  The winners of the competition were




Drone Show:
A drone show was arranged for the students' entertainment at cricket ground at 2pm after the bike and car expo. Dr. A. S. Bhalachandra, Prasad Kokil, S. P. Atipamulu, S. D. Gadhire, and other faculty members were also present for the show along with a huge crowd of students. In this event, drones and their capabilities were exhibited along with the vibrant smoke show.




Summit:
The GECCS MUN organized the Summit in Wings 2024 along with the team of Wings. The theme of the summit was technology, innovation, investment, enterpreneurship and business. The event was divided into two sessions of 2 hours each.




Session 1:
The first session that started at 01:30 PM was moderated by Mr. Samey Lyngrah and Ms. Rahi Gaikwad. The panelists for this particular session were Mr. Alok Mukharjee ( Scientist, DRDO), Mr. Sharanya Giri ( Youtuber, Founder, Dronology drone company), Mr. Mukund Kulkarni ( Board of Management member GECCS, Director at Expert Global Solutions), Mr. Prasad Kokil ( Board of Management member GECCS, Director at Sanjay Technoplast), Mr. Krishna Paradhi ( Vice-President at Laxmi Group of Industries) and the Principal of our college Prof. Dr. Sanjay Dambhare.

Session 2:
The second session started at 4:00 pm with Ms. Sanchi Rampurkar and Mr. Anurang Sarda as the moderators. The panelists for the session were Mr. Siraj Shaikh ( Deputy General Manager at Cotton Greaves ), Mr. Goutam Choudhary ( Data Scientist at Tata Skills) and our Principal sir. The event was a grand success with the presence of a large number of students and faculties.





Day 3:


Zumba Dance:
On 21st of January, 2024 a Zumba session was arranged for the students and faculty members in the morning at 8 am to 9:30 am. Around 50 to 60 students along with some faculty members like Dr. A. S. Bhalachandra, Dr. S. B. Nemade, Prof. V. P. Gaikwad, Prof. Sunil Shastri and Prof. S. P. Atipamulu were present for the Zumba session. Mr. Sunil Kamble was the instructor for the Zumba session. Dr. A. S. Bhalachandra felicitated him at the start of the session. The students as well as the faculty members enjoyed the zumba dance.




Esports:
Esports was the newest addition to the list of competitions which included a competition of video games like Valorant and BGMI. It was conducted at Electrical Seminar hall and Electronics Seminar hall at 8 am to 5:30 pm. 10 teams participated for Valorant in the first round. For the second round, top 4 teams were selected for Valorant. The winners were congratulated and felicitated.




Keynote session by Akshansh Kataria:
A keynote session on Thermal and Fluid Energy by Akshansh Kataria was conducted from 9:30 am to 11:30 am. He shared about the different utensils Ecosense makes, the eco chula, and also the basics principles on which the internal combustion chamber operates . At the end of his talk, he motivated and urged the students to partake in more eco-friendly and sustainable technologies as it is the need of our time to switch to such clean alternatives to the traditional technologies.





Keynote session by Rahul Patle:
A keynote session on Rahul Patle was conducted from 11:30 am to 1:30 pm. Mr. Patle then shared his experience  about drones and about the latest innovation as well as the growing demand for drones in today’s world. At the end of his talk, he motivated and urged the students to partake in more innovative projects as well as drone technology as these are the new and upcoming fields with massive scope.




Robo Race:
Robo race was arranged at Kho Kho ground from 1:30 pm to 5 pm. A total of 19 teams participated in the competition out of which 6 were from our college and 13 were from all over Maharashtra. All the teams had to cross the race track with all the hurdles and showcase their robots and their robot handling skills. A total of top five winners were felicitated and rewarded.




Prize Distribution and Valediction:
The prize distribution cceremony was conducted at 6:30 pm on the last day of Wings 2024. The winners of all the competitions were felicitated and rewarded in the ceremony. Robo race: 1st Place: Rahul kr Sangwan, Northcap University, Gurgaon – Rs. 11,000 2nd Place: Ayush Jindal, Northcap University, Gurgaon – Rs. 7,000 3rd Place: Rohit Shelar, Nashik – Rs. 5,000 4th Place: Pratik Wayal, GECCS – Rs. 4,000 5th Place: Jivan Bhutkar, GECCS – Rs. 3,000
